Kerry Spina

Kerry Spina

My husband inspired my wholefood journey. I'd had what I thought were chronic sinus issues every morning for the past 10 years but found when I removed dairy and wheat from my diet that my symptoms just disappeared! I then became committed to a more whole food approach. My goal is for my diet to be 80% wholefoods and 20% whatever I feel like or is available as I don't want to feel trapped by my own rules. That's why I adore N.B! I get to have a beautiful, nourishing treat and feel good in my body and mind without comprising my health and without feeling like I'm missing out on a treat.

INTERESTED IN BECOMING A STOCKIST?

We currently serve over 30 wholesale customers including cafes, health food stores and supermarkets throughout Queensland and are looking to extend this further to retailers in New South Wales and Victoria. We would be delighted to add your business to our list!

We make our delicious goodies fresh to order each week in our gluten free production facility and deliver them to you via refrigerated transport. You also have the option of receiving our products unpackaged in a partitioned box or in individual retail-ready product packaging.

And if you’re not sure what to order, we’d be happy to send you a complimentary box of samples so you can try our delicious treats. We know you’ll love them just as much as we do.
